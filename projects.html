Pacioli: Full-Stack Crypto ERP (Coming Soon)

Bringing triple-entry accounting and institutional-grade compliance to the decentralized economy.

Named after the father of double-entry bookkeeping, Pacioli is an open-source ERP and accounting framework designed to solve the complexity of digital asset-native financial management.

By integrating real-time blockchain telemetry with traditional accounting standards, Pacioli enables non-profits and decentralized organizations to manage digital assets with the same rigor as legacy fiat systems. It is the financial infrastructure required to build trust in global philanthropy.

  • Verifiable Transparency: Automated reconciliation of on-chain transactions to sub-ledger accounts.
  • Compliance-First Architecture: Designed for 501(c)(3) reporting requirements and international regulatory standards.
